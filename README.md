# Project Overview

This project performs comprehensive sales analysis on Walmart's historical data to answer important business questions such as:

- Which stores generate the highest revenue?
- How do holidays affect weekly sales?
- Is temperature related to sales performance?
- What sales trends exist over time?
- How can future sales be forecasted?
- What business strategies can improve revenue?

The project simulates a real-world business analytics workflow by combining Python analysis, SQL querying, dashboard development, and predictive forecasting.

---

# Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing stores
- Measure holiday sales impact
- Study temperature and sales relationship
- Forecast future weekly sales
- Generate business recommendations
- Build interactive business dashboards

# Key Insights

## Sales Trends

- Weekly sales remain relatively stable across most months.
- Significant sales spikes occur during holiday periods.

---

## Top Stores

Top-performing stores include

- Store 20
- Store 4
- Store 14

These stores contribute a substantial share of total revenue.

---

## Holiday Analysis

- Holiday weeks have slightly higher average weekly sales.
- Most revenue is still generated during non-holiday weeks due to their larger number.

---

## Economic Factors

- Temperature has a weak relationship with sales.
- CPI and unemployment show limited direct impact on weekly sales.

---

## Forecast

The Prophet forecasting model predicts relatively stable future sales with expected seasonal fluctuations.

Forecast Period:

52 Weeks

# Business Recommendations

- Replicate successful strategies from top-performing stores.
